# Rimba ERP

### Resource Integration for Manufacturing & Business Alignment

**One Platform. One Organization. Complete Operational Visibility.**

Rimba ERP is an integrated manufacturing management platform designed to connect people, processes, assets, knowledge, compliance, and execution into a single system. Instead of purchasing and integrating multiple disconnected applications, manufacturers can manage their entire organization from one platform.

---

## Why Rimba?

Manufacturing companies often struggle with:

* Multiple disconnected systems
* Manual spreadsheets and paperwork
* Poor visibility across departments
* Compliance and audit challenges
* Lost organizational knowledge
* Inefficient approval processes
* Workforce planning difficulties

Rimba provides a unified operational backbone that connects every department and business process.

## Modular Yet Unified

Rimba consists of integrated modules including:

| Module                 | Purpose                                  |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Workforce Planning     | Organization, positions, roles, capacity |
| HR Management          | Employee lifecycle                       |
| Workflow Engine        | Process automation                       |
| Task Management        | Operational execution                    |
| Learning Management    | Training and certification               |
| Document Management    | Knowledge and records                    |
| Asset Management       | Equipment lifecycle                      |
| Maintenance Management | Preventive and corrective maintenance    |
| Risk Management        | Risk identification and mitigation       |
| CAPA Management        | Corrective and preventive actions        |
| Contract Management    | Legal and commercial agreements          |
| Vendor Management      | Supplier lifecycle                       |

All modules share the same organizational structure, permissions, workflows, and audit framework.
