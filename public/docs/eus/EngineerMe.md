# Rimba Community

## Vision

Rimba aims to become the operational digital backbone for manufacturing organizations.

We welcome developers, manufacturers, consultants, trainers, auditors, and industry specialists to participate in building the ecosystem.

---

## Architecture Principles

### Open Core

The foundation platform remains openly available to encourage adoption and community participation.

### Modular Design

Capabilities are delivered through independent modules that can evolve without affecting the entire platform.

### API First

Every capability should be accessible through well-defined APIs.

### Event Driven

Modules communicate through events to promote flexibility and scalability.

### Multi Organization

The platform is designed to support multiple organizations and business units.
