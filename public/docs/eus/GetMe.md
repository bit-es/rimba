# Rimba Platform

## Overview

Rimba is built as a modular operational platform for manufacturing organizations.

Organizations can start with core capabilities and progressively enable additional modules as business needs evolve.

---

## Foundation Platform

Included in the Community Edition:

* Authentication
* Authorization
* Organization Management
* Workforce Planning
* Workflow Engine
* Task Management
* Calendar & Scheduling
* Document Management
* API Framework

These services form the operational backbone of the platform.

## Editions

### Community Edition

Free and open-source foundation platform.

### Professional Edition

Designed for growing manufacturers requiring workforce, supplier, and knowledge management capabilities.

### Enterprise Edition

Designed for organizations requiring governance, maintenance, risk, compliance, and advanced planning capabilities.
